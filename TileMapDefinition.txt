The tilemap definition:
tilemap is .json file

ObjectType: use number to represent the type of the object,
0 is Mario
1 is Goomba
2 is KoopaTroopa
3 is QuestionBlock
4 is UsedBlock
5 is BrickBlock
6 is FloorBlock
7 is PyramidBlock
8 is Pipe
9 is SuperMushroom
10 is OneUpMushroom
12 is FireFlower
13 Starman

RectangleX, RectangleY: Location of the Object 
RectangleHeight, RectangleWidth: Size of the Object
